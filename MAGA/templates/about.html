Envisioning A Greener Australia


About Us

Australia's Waste is an ever-growing issue that millions of Australians face in their everyday life. Although, unlike several diseases, economic crisis and mental health issues, the effects of the growing waste aren't readily apparent.

Watch a short video about the project, the background and the need for a change



Our Purpose

The project 'Make Australia Green Again' was established to assist in educating parents of young children who want to improve their recycling habits for a better tomorrow. We believe in leaving behind a sustainable and green Australia for the future Australians so they can enjoy this beautiful country.'

Our Targets

The project is aimed at young couples focused on improving their recycling habits and reducing their carbon footprint. We aim at achieving the following milestones:

  • Cleaning Australian Recycle one household at a time
  • Educating parents and children on proper recycling habits
  • Providing assistance in waste classification

Our Hope

We hope to improve the life of future Australians by bestowing upon them a sense of proper recycling habits which they can carry over to the future. We hope you enjoy using the product and stay tuned for future updates!
