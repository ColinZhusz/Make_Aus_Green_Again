What We Do

Australia's ever-growing waste problem has caused significant environmental and monetary setbacks to the Australian population. Even though it's not readily apparent, the growing waste issue concerns every citizen of this beautiful country.

Since January 2018, China has stopped accepting Australia's recycle due to impurities, causing taxpayers millions. The main issue being contaminants being present in Australia's recycle due to improper waste classification.

Our solution is to provide an interactive way to classify waste correctly with a little magic of Machine Learning. Our tool will help educate the audience on the correct way to dispose of several daily items. The users of our products will get a customized solution matching their needs and be able to become better, more responsible citizens of Australia.
